Silent Night

Monster Slayer

You push yourself behind the door, trying your best to be invisible. Soon enough, the killer enters the room. With his back turned to you, he doesn’t notice you. You lunge! And your knife sinks in between his shoulder blades! He falls. He drops his weapon, as well. You scoop it up and runs out of your room, two knives, one bloody and one not, in your hands. You don’t spare another glance at the dying man on your bedroom floor. You can’t bring yourself too, lest it makes you throw up. You sit in the living room for the remainder of the time it takes the police to get here. Within seven minutes, you saw the telltale signs of red and blue lights. Finally.
